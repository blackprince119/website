Wendy's Meals

Baconator ($14.99)

The Baconator is a sandwich made by Wendy's restaurants. It has two beef patties, two slices of American cheese, six strips of bacon, mayonnaise, and ketchup on a bun.

Krabby Patty ($22.99)

This limited-edition burger features a quarter-pound Wendy's burger, two slices of melted American cheese, all the classic toppings—lettuce, tomato, pickles and onions—and a top-secret Krabby Patty sauce that brings it all together.

Frosty ($4.99)

With a consistency that falls somewhere between a milkshake and soft-serve ice cream, the Frosty was created to be eaten with a spoon. But if you prefer to let your Frosty melt a bit to enjoy it with a straw, we won't stop you.
